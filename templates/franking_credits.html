Dividends & Franking Credits

Record dividend income and attached franking credits (imputation credits) per financial year. Used in the FY Summary and tax export.

What are franking credits? Australian companies pay 30% corporate tax on profits before paying dividends. As a shareholder you can claim this as a tax credit (franking credit) against your income tax assessment. A fully-franked $700 dividend on a 30% rate grosses up to $1,000 with a $300 credit. If your marginal rate is lower than 30%, you receive the excess credit as a refund.
