Account Reconciliation

Compare the running balance in the database against the closing balance captured from statement imports.

How reconciliation works

For each account, PFA computes the sum of all transactions in the database up to the date of the most recent balance snapshot. It then compares this to the closing balance recorded when the statement was imported.

OK — variance < $0.02 (rounding only).
Warning — variance between $0.02 and $10: minor gap, possibly a missed small transaction.
Error — variance over $10: a transaction may be missing or duplicated.
No snapshot — no closing balance was captured for this account (ANZ Plus and Revolut statements provide this; others do not).
