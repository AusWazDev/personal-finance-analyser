How to use the Reports section

Interactive charts built from all your transaction data. Every chart responds instantly to the period filter.

  • Filter period — use the dropdown above to narrow all charts to any date range: this month, a financial year, last 90 days, or a custom range. Charts update instantly with no server call.
  • Click a category bar in Spend by Category or Top Merchants to jump directly to the Transactions page, pre-filtered to that category and the current date range.
  • Income vs Expenditure — monthly grouped bar showing all income streams vs total spend.
  • Spend by Category — total spend per category for the period, sorted largest first. Click any bar to drill down.
  • Budget vs Actual — compares your configured monthly budgets against actual average spend. Red bars exceed budget.
  • Spend Trends — monthly line chart for your top 8 spending categories.
  • Recurring — merchants appearing in 2 or more months, sorted by total spend.
  • Top Merchants — top 20 individual merchants by total spend. Click any bar to see all transactions from that merchant.
  • Day of Week — heatmap showing which days of the week you spend the most across each month.
  • Business Expenses — all transactions flagged as business expenses, for reimbursement tracking.
